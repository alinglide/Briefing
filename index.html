Reporting of defects and incidents

The appointed person should ensure that there is an effective procedure for reporting defects and incidents. This should include immediate notification of:

  • Any defects found during daily and weekly checks.
  • Defects found at any other time.
  • Incidents, accidents or near misses however slight.
  • Shock loads, however, they occur.
  • Dangerous occurrence and reportable accidents.
  • Report any radio communication issues to the principal contractor.
